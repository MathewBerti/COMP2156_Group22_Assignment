George Brown College's Computer Systems Technology program provides comprehensive training in network administration, cybersecurity, and 
IT infrastructure management. Students gain practical experience through hands-on labs and real-world projects, 
preparing them for roles as network technicians, system administrators, or cybersecurity analysts. 
With a focus on emerging technologies and industry certifications, 
graduates are well-equipped to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving IT sector

Furthermore, the program emphasizes critical thinking and problem-solving skills,
 enabling students to effectively troubleshoot complex network and security issues
